About the System

The Campus Lost & Found System helps students report lost belongings, submit found items, and search for missing valuables within the campus community. It makes recovering lost items simple, quick, and convenient.

  • Clear lost and found report forms
  • Item listings for easier matching
  • Campus office contact information
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
